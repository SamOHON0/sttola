Our Story

From a kitchen table to the world.

St Tola Goat Cheese was made originally by Meg and Derrick Gordon. Their neighbour and long-time friend Siobhán Ní Gháirbhith took over the business in 1999, and has grown it from a cottage industry to an internationally recognised brand employing eight people. Read our story below.

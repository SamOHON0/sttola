Recognition

Quietly collecting trophies. For a quarter of a century.

From the World Cheese Awards to the tables of Michelin-listed kitchens, St Tola has been recognised at home and abroad. A few of the moments we are proudest of.

2025

Georgina Campbell Food Tourism Experience

The first ever winner of this award, for welcoming visitors to the farm to meet the herd and taste the cheese.

2025

World Cheese Awards Super Gold

One of the most prestigious recognitions in global cheesemaking.

2026

Featured at the Michelin Guide Awards

Showcased on La Rousse Foods' Irish display at the Michelin Guide Awards UK & Ireland.

2024

BBC feature with Liz Bonnin & Anna Haugh

Siobhán hosted Liz Bonnin and chef Anna Haugh on the farm for the BBC.

Past

Served at The Savoy, London

St Tola has graced the menus of some of the finest restaurants in Britain and Ireland.

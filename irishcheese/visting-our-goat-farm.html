Come see for yourself

Visit the farm. Meet the goats.

We welcome tours of all kinds to our beautiful 65-acre farm on the edge of the Burren.

Our tour takes about an hour and a half and includes a full walk of the working farm. There is a chance to pet the goats, see them at feeding time, and get a real understanding of sustainable farming. A cheese-making demonstration is included, along with a tasting of our gourmet cheeses.

Public tours, July 2026

  • Wednesdays 1st, 8th, 22nd and 29th July at 10.30am
  • €15 per head, or €45 for a family of four
  • You may also join pre-booked tours when space allows, at €15 per head

Private tours

Private tours can be arranged for groups of any size, with a Premium tour and an Ultimate Experience available. Bookable at 10.30am, 12.30pm and 2.30pm. The minimum cost for a private tour is €240. Email info@st-tola.ie for the full range of tours and prices.

Good to know

  • 2026 tours run Tuesdays, Wednesdays and Fridays only
  • No tours on weekends or bank holidays
  • All tours must be booked in advance — we are a busy working farm, so places are limited

Where to find us

Our farm is just south of the Burren and only half an hour from the Cliffs of Moher, so it slots easily into a full day touring Co Clare. Eircode V95 XA9C.

We are proud members of the Burren and Cliffs of Moher UNESCO Global Geopark and its GEOfood network, the Burren Ecotourism Network and Food Trail, and Économusée, the craft-producer visitor scheme. Together they celebrate a food tradition rooted in this landscape.
